# Diet and Nutrition

Aggregated findings across all Huberman Lab episodes on this topic.


- AMH (anti-müllerian hormone) testing is crucial for all women who may want children someday—it measures egg quantity, not quality, and provides essential data for family planning decisions. — *from [EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility &amp; Hormone Health | Dr. Natalie Crawford](../Episodes/EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility and Hormone Health - Dr. Natalie Crawford/summary.md)*
- Fertility is a vital health marker that extends far beyond pregnancy; infertility correlates with increased rates of metabolic syndrome, cancer, heart attack, stroke, and early death, making it a window into chronic inflammation and metabolic dysfunction. — *from [EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility &amp; Hormone Health | Dr. Natalie Crawford](../Episodes/EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility and Hormone Health - Dr. Natalie Crawford/summary.md)*
- Women with infertility have higher rates of chronic inflammation and insulin resistance—the underlying drivers are often detectable and treatable before they progress to serious disease. — *from [EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility &amp; Hormone Health | Dr. Natalie Crawford](../Episodes/EP-391 - How Women Can Improve Their Fertility and Hormone Health - Dr. Natalie Crawford/summary.md)*
