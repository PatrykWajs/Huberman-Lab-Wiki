# Episode 169: AMA #12: Thoughts on Longevity Supplements (Resveratrol, NR, NMN, Etc.) & How to Improve Memory

> 📄 [View Full Transcript](transcript.md)

## Episode Overview

This is a premium preview (~27 minutes) of AMA #12, released October 31, 2023. Andrew Huberman addresses a listener question about resveratrol and uses it as a launching point to survey the major longevity supplement categories: resveratrol and grape seed extract, the NAD pathway (NR, NMN, and IV NAD infusions), metformin, berberine, mTOR, and rapamycin. He shares his personal protocols and the rationale behind each choice, drawing a firm distinction between supplements taken for vitality versus those claimed to extend lifespan — a claim he finds unsupported by current human evidence across the board. The full premium episode also covers working memory improvement (not included in the public preview).

## Key Takeaways

- **Resveratrol does not extend lifespan in humans.** By 2023 Huberman considers the lifespan-extension hypothesis largely debunked — no convincing human, primate, or recent mouse data exist. He does not take resveratrol. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Grape seed extract is taken for vascular function, not longevity.** Huberman takes 400–800 mg/day with a meal for blood flow and vascular health. He explicitly states this is not for lifespan extension and would not rank it in his top 10 supplements overall. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **NAD declines with age; NR and NMN are precursors used to raise it.** The NAD pathway is central to cellular energetics and mitochondrial function. NR reliably raises NAD; NMN has ongoing debate about cellular uptake and conversion efficiency. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Huberman takes NR and NMN daily — for subjective energy, not lifespan.** He takes 500 mg NR and 1–2 g sublingual NMN every morning before his first meal (~11 am). The effect is sustained mental and physical energy through the day that tapers off by evening. He has no financial relationship with any NR or NMN manufacturer. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **NMN was reclassified by the FDA in 2023.** Because a company filed NMN as an investigational drug in a clinical trial, the FDA removed it from the legal supplement category. It remains available on Amazon and elsewhere but purity is unverified. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **NAD IV infusions directly raise cellular NAD but are uncomfortable and expensive.** Huberman has done this twice (500–1,000 mg IV), always post-illness or when run-down, experiencing improved sleep and vigor afterward. Side effects during infusion — nausea, chest pressure, leg pressure — typically resolve within 10 minutes. Slower drip rate reduces discomfort. Do infusions earlier in the day to avoid sleep disruption. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Metformin: insufficient peer-reviewed evidence for lifespan extension.** After reviewing the data with Dr. Peter Attia on a joint journal-club episode, Huberman finds no sufficient basis to take metformin for longevity. He does not take it. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Berberine causes brutal headaches for Huberman personally.** He attributes this to blood-glucose lowering and avoids it entirely, while also being skeptical of its longevity claims in humans. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **mTOR is the common theoretical target of metformin, berberine, and rapamycin.** mTOR (mammalian Target of Rapamycin) is abundant in growing/young cells and declines with age. The logic that reducing mTOR extends lifespan lacks direct human evidence. Huberman has personally researched mTOR extensively in the context of visual-system regeneration. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Rapamycin: biologically promising but side-effect profile warrants caution.** Researchers like Dr. Matt Kaeberlein (Dog Aging Project) and Dr. Peter Attia are actively exploring it. Huberman does not currently take it — insufficient human lifespan data and a meaningful side-effect profile are his stated reasons. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
- **Lifestyle fundamentals dwarf any supplement for longevity.** No pill, capsule, or infusion approaches the longevity benefit of quality sleep, zone 2 cardio (180–220 min/week), VO2 max training (at least once/week), resistance training (6+ sets per muscle group/week), quality nutrition, stress modulation, social connection, and morning sunlight. Supplements are enhancements within safety and budget constraints — not the foundation. [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)

## Tools & Protocols

### Grape Seed Extract
- **What:** 400–800 mg grape seed extract
- **How:** Take with any meal
- **When:** Daily
- **Why:** Vascular function and blood flow — not longevity or resveratrol-pathway effects
- **Ref:** [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)

### NR + NMN Stack
- **What:** 500 mg NR (nicotinamide riboside) + 1–2 g NMN (nicotinamide mononucleotide) sublingual powder
- **How:** NMN placed under tongue until dissolved; NR taken as capsule or powder at the same time
- **When:** Morning, within 1–2 hours of waking, at least 30 minutes before first meal (~11 am for Huberman)
- **Why:** Raise NAD levels for sustained mental and physical energy — not for lifespan extension
- **Ref:** [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)

### NAD IV Infusion (Occasional)
- **What:** 500–1,000 mg NAD administered intravenously
- **How:** At a clinic or mobile service; request slower drip rate to reduce nausea and pressure sensations; expect ~45–60 min infusion time; anti-nausea meds available but Huberman declined them
- **When:** Occasional — a few times per year when run-down or post-illness; always earlier in the day to protect sleep
- **Why:** Direct cellular NAD repletion; Huberman reports improved sleep and vigor post-infusion
- **Ref:** [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)

### Longevity Lifestyle Foundation
- **Zone 2 cardio:** Minimum 180–220 minutes per week at conversational intensity
- **VO2 max training:** At least once per week — push heart rate to near-maximum
- **Resistance training:** Minimum 6 sets per muscle group per week; sufficient intensity to maintain muscle size, strength, and nerve-to-muscle connectivity (correlates with cognitive function)
- **Sleep:** Single most important longevity behavior per Huberman
- **Morning sunlight:** Daily, to anchor circadian rhythm
- **Quality nutrition, stress modulation, social connection:** All foundational before any supplementation
- **Ref:** [00:01:46](transcript.md#000146-resveratrol-and-longevity-supplements)
